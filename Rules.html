General rules of Baseball
Baseball is a very complex game to many it's boring becasue people dont fully understand whats going on. There are 2 two teams of 9 players on each team. A game constist of innings typically 7 or 9 there is no time limit. An inning is split into two halves top half the way team hits and the bottom half the home team hits. You switch half innings when the team playing defensegets 3 outs. The way a team scores is by a a batter running to every base 1st, 2nd, 3rd,and then home plate.
