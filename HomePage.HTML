Welcome to the world of Baseball

Baseball has been around since 1839 and is often referred to as America's past time. Baseball today is one of the biggest sports in the country. According to Statista in 2017 over 15 million people played the sport from ages 6 and up.


Reasons to Love Baseball

  • Its Played all around the world, there is even a world baseball classic every 4 years
  • The MLB is the second most popular professional sport in the US
  • You can start playing at 5 and play until you physically cant
  • Unlike football and basketball you dont need to be some freak athlete to be succesful
