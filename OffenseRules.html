Offense Rules
As a batter you go up to the plate and have to try and hit the ball off the pitcher before he gets 3 strikes. A strike is a pitch that is in a zone that is from the top of the batters knees to the middle of his torso, and goes the with of homeplate. If you get to 3 strikes in one at bat then youre automatically out. If you swing the pitch is automatically a strike and if you foul it off meaning you hit the ball that isnt inside the white lines that are on the field then that is a strike as well, but you can not strike out on a foul ball. If you already have 2 strike on you then you continue to have 2 stike till you either put the ball in play, the pitchers thorws a stike that you do not make contact with. 4 Ways to get on base
  1. Hit

    • Double- The Batter make it to second
    • Triple- The Batter makes it too third
    • Homerun- The Batter hits the ball over the fense and goes all the way home
  2. Walk or Hit by Pitcher
    • A walk is when a pitcher thorow 4 balls that arent a strike the batter goes to first for free
    • If the the pitcher hits the batter with the ball then the batter goes to first for free
  3. Error
    • The batter hits the ball and the defense make a bad play and deosnt get an out
The difference between a hit and an error is if the player is given a hit then the defense didnt have any chance to get the batter out. An error is the batter hits right to a player on defense and they make a bad throw or the ball rolls between their legs.
